1. Information We Collect

When you register and use NEETMINDS™, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Profile information: Category (General/OBC/SC/ST), target exam year, and optional location
  • Usage data: Questions attempted, accuracy rates, time spent per session, pattern performance, and analytics data
  • Payment information: Transaction records processed via Razorpay. We do not store card numbers or banking credentials — these are handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant systems
  • Device & technical data: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and session identifiers (via cookies)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform and its features
  • Personalise your experience — including adaptive practice recommendations based on your performance
  • Process your subscription and send billing-related communications
  • Send you important service notices (account, payment, policy updates)
  • Respond to your support requests and enquiries
  • Analyse aggregate usage trends to improve Platform quality
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

3. Cookies

We use cookies (small text files stored on your device) for the following purposes:

  • Session cookies: To keep you logged in during a session
  • Preference cookies: To remember settings such as dark mode and category selection
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how users navigate the Platform and where improvements are needed

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, doing so may affect certain Platform features such as staying logged in or retaining preferences.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information only with trusted service providers who help us operate the Platform:

  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • Cloud hosting providers — secure server infrastructure based in India
  • Analytics tools — aggregate, anonymised usage data only

We may also disclose your information if required by law, by a court order, or to protect the rights and safety of NEETMINDS™ or its users.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information — including SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit, secure password hashing, and access controls limiting who within our team can access user data.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no digital system is completely immune to security risks. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you promptly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active, or as required to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records (e.g. billing records for tax purposes — retained for 7 years as per Indian law).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on the Platform. Continued use of the Platform after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
